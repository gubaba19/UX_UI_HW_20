Ideation Design

Design System The wireframes, style guide, and prototypes were all made inside Miro and Figma.

The green color palette was chosen because of it's association with peace and security. The B612 font-style was chosen because of the positive effect on reducing visual fatigue and cognitive load. This is the same typeface used on aircrafts.
